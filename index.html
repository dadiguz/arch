About Me

Architect

I'm an architect with Mexican roots and training. My identity lies in the cultural aspects of growing up with two countries to call home.

Those factors molded me and defined my architectural style. Dualism and bilingualism are things I've engrained and take pride in.

Creating a rich ambiance — finding a balance between the constructed space, its surroundings and context — is what I believe makes any architectonic work complete.
