  **Long Play — Lighting intent:**
  Crafted to evoke the warmth and moody ambiance of a mid-century vintage shop. The design features a combination of track lighting and pendants with a retro flair, emitting a soft, warm glow that highlights the vinyl records and creates an inviting atmosphere. The lighting levels are carefully balanced to provide an adequate view of the products while maintaining a cozy, intimate feel. The overall aesthetic is a nod to the classic record stores of the past, with a modern twist.
  - Mid-century modern-inspired fixtures in warm finishes (brass, copper, wood)
  - Soft, warm LED lighting (2700K–3000K) to reduce glare and create a relaxing ambiance
  - Strategically placed track lighting to highlight featured records and create visual interest
  - Dimmable lighting controls to adjust ambiance according to the time of day or special events

  **Transmisiones González — Lighting intent:**
  Paying homage to mid-century style with a modern twist. The intent is to create a welcoming and functional atmosphere that provides clear visibility for customers and staff.
  - Mid-century inspired fixtures with metal and glass finishes
  - Cool white LED lighting (3500K–4000K) for enhanced visibility
  - Focused lighting on work areas and product displays
  - Adequate lighting levels for an automotive shop, with a minimum of 500 lux in work areas

  **Mariscos Playa Azul — Design intent:**
  Comprehensive design package celebrating its rich history. The lighting and interior design blend vintage charm with modern functionality, creating a lively, beachy atmosphere that honors the restaurant's legacy.
  - Warm, inviting lighting (2700K–3000K) with a mix of pendant lights, sconces, and table lamps
  - Reclaimed wood accents, nautical elements, and vintage decor to evoke the spirit of a classic coastal eatery
  - Adequate lighting levels: 300–500 lux in dining areas and 500–700 lux in food prep areas
  - Comfortable seating, natural textures, and coastal colors to create a welcoming ambiance

  **TJ Water — Design intent:**
  Crafted to be modern, clean, and functional. The goal is to create a welcoming and efficient space for customers and staff.
  - Bright, neutral LED lighting (3500K–4000K) for clarity and visibility
  - Sleek, minimalist fixtures that complement the dispensary's modern aesthetic
  - Adequate lighting levels: 500–700 lux in service areas and 300–500 lux in customer areas

  **Casa Paulina — Design intent:**
  A serene retreat that embodies an introspective architectural style, blending traditional Mexican chukum plaster with mid-century modern elements. The design wraps around a multi-level layout, creating a sense of safety and tranquility.
  - Chukum plaster walls and ceilings, providing a warm, earthy texture and natural beauty
  - Abundant natural light, filtered through strategically placed windows and skylights
  - Mid-century modern accents: clean lines, organic shapes, and functional elegance
  - Multiple levels, creating a sense of depth and visual interest
  - Warm, inviting lighting (2700K–3000K) that highlights the chukum texture and creates a cozy atmosphere
